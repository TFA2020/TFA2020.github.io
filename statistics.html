When asked "New York City residents receive translation services...", 57.3% of survey respondents indicated the wrong answer and are therefore not aware of their right to translation services. The answer choices were


  • As a right from the government (correct answer)
  • At the discretion of the agency
  • None of the time

    • When asked "I have received adequate translation services, when needed, at my healthcare facility", 54.5% of survey respondents disagreed or strongly disagreed with the claim. More than half of the survey respondents report receiving inadequate translation services.
